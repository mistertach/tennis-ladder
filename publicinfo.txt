The Dhahran Tennis Association (DTA) is a vibrant community dedicated to promoting the sport of tennis within Dhahran, Saudi Arabia. Whether you are a seasoned competitor or picking up a racket for the first time, our association provides the structure, coaching, and social environment needed to enjoy the game at any level.2026 Spring SchedulePlease note that these dates are subject to change. Most activities occur at the Dhahran Hills tennis courts.Spring Leagues: Weekly sessions running from January 18 through May 24 (except during Ramadan).Aramco Sport Championship (ASC) Qualifiers: January 4 – February 17.Ramadan Mini-leagues: February 22 – March 18.Aramco Sport Championship Finals: April 10–25.Camel Cup: April 26 – May 2 (Dates TBD).End-of-season Social: May 16.General League RulesPunctuality: Players must show up on time; warm-ups should be kept to roughly 10 minutes so play can begin by 5:10 p.m..Equipment: One can of balls is provided per court from Coach Desta (usually near Court 1 or 14) and must be returned after matches.Weather: Do not assume play is cancelled due to weather unless notified; players should meet at the courts to decide.Substitutes: Players are responsible for finding their own substitutes if they cannot attend. Your ranking cannot go up based on a sub's performance, only down or stay the same.No-Shows: A no-show results in moving down a group, and a first offense includes a 6-game reduction in the next week's score.Mixed Singles SpecificsFormat: Each player in a group plays a round-robin of 7-game sets against three opponents.Tiebreaks: If a set reaches 3-3, a tiebreak is played for the 7th game (first to 7, win by two; if it reaches 7-7, the first to 9 wins).Movement: The player with the most total games won moves up one group; the player with the least moves down. If one player beats all others in the group, they move up regardless of total game count.Mixed Doubles SpecificsFormat: Players complete three sets by playing a round-robin with 8 games per set.Scoring: Regular deuce is used, but there are no tiebreakers even at 4-4.No-Shows: If only three players arrive, "Canadian doubles" is played so that everyone still gets three sets.Movement: Similar to singles, the player with the most total games won moves up a group, and the one with the least moves down.League OpportunitiesThe DTA offers a diverse range of leagues:Mixed Singles & Mixed Doubles Leagues.Jadid League: For beginners.Women’s Double League & Morning Singles League.WhatsApp CommunityMembers gain access to a managed WhatsApp community featuring:League-Specific Groups for score reporting and subs.Q&A with the Board.Coaching Group for professional coaching sessions.Membership and FeesMembership TypeAnnual Fee (SAR)Individual150 SARFamily200 SARRegistration: Complete the DTA Registration Form.2025 DTA Board MembersPresident: Daniel | VP: Zara | Secretary: Robert | Treasurer: JohnMembership: Amy | Mx Doubles: Vadzim | Mx Singles: Omar TWomen’s D: Britta/Jamie | Women’s Singles: Sandi | Tourney: Mary/ThuySocial: Samira | Facilities: Susan | Coaching: Steve
